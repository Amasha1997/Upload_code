Engineered marvel at its best

Since an era bygone, Man has had an enigmatic relationship with stone. It struck a chord somewhere and then began a tumultuous affair. Drawing inspiration from this, PM Quartz designs and manufactures luxurious and premium quartz surfaces that are used to adorn interiors and kitchen countertops. Founded in 2015, PM Quartz is a subsidiary of PM Granite Export Private Limited, which is an export oriented unit, who processes exquisite natural granite surfaces.

PMB group is a conglomerate with its headquarters in Bangalore. PM Quartz works on surfaces and falls under the umbrella of PMB Group. Under the guidance of Shri. M. Babanna, a well-known industrialist with three decades of experience, PMB Group is a conglomerate that has grown multi-fold and diversified.
